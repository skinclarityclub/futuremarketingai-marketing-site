## Posting Guidelines

### Timing

- **Best times NL:** Tuesday-Thursday 8:00-9:00 AM CET, or 12:00-1:00 PM CET
- **Weekend posts:** Sunday evening (7:00-8:00 PM) for Monday morning feed
- **Avoid:** Friday afternoon, Saturday

### Engagement protocol

- Reply to every comment within 2 hours of posting
- Ask follow-up questions in replies (keeps the thread active)
- Comment on 5-10 prospect posts daily (substantive, not "Great post!")
- Like and reply to comments from target agency owners

### Hashtag strategy

- Maximum 3-5 hashtags per post
- Primary: #AIMarketing #MarketingBureau #MarketingAutomation
- Secondary: #DigitalMarketing #AgencyLife #MarTech
- Do not use trending or unrelated hashtags

### Metrics to track

| Metric               | Target (Week 1) | Target (Week 2) |
| -------------------- | --------------- | --------------- |
| Impressions per post | 500+            | 1,000+          |
| Engagement rate      | 3-5%            | 5-8%            |
| Profile views        | 50+/week        | 100+/week       |
| Connection requests  | 10+/week        | 20+/week        |
| DM conversations     | 2-3             | 5-8             |

### Content bank (backup topics)

If a scheduled post does not feel right, swap with one of these:

- "De ROI berekening: AI medewerker vs junior marketeer" (data post)
- "5 signalen dat je bureau klaar is voor AI" (listicle)
- "Waarom ik geen marketing automation tool bouw" (contrarian)
- "Het verschil tussen AI tools en een AI medewerker" (educational)
- "Wat ik leerde van 50 gesprekken met bureau-eigenaren" (personal story)
