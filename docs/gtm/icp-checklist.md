# ICP Qualification Checklist

Use this checklist to score every prospective agency before adding them to the outreach pipeline. Each criterion scores 0-2 points for a maximum of 12.

## Scoring Criteria

### 1. Team Size

| Score | Criteria           |
| ----- | ------------------ |
| 2     | 3-12 FTE           |
| 1     | 13-25 FTE          |
| 0     | 1-2 FTE or 26+ FTE |

**Why:** Agencies with 3-12 people feel scaling pain but lack budget for full hires. Too small means no scaling need; too large means enterprise buying process.

### 2. Focus Area

| Score | Criteria                                                      |
| ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2     | Performance marketing, growth marketing, or content marketing |
| 1     | Mixed services (performance + branding)                       |
| 0     | Branding-only or creative-only                                |

**Why:** Performance and growth agencies have repeatable processes that AI can automate. Branding agencies rely on human creativity and subjective taste.

### 3. LinkedIn Activity (Founder/Owner)

| Score | Criteria                                |
| ----- | --------------------------------------- |
| 2     | Founder posts weekly or more            |
| 1     | Founder posts monthly                   |
| 0     | Founder inactive (no posts in 30+ days) |

**Why:** Active LinkedIn presence means the founder is reachable via the outreach sequence and values digital presence. Inactive founders are harder to engage.

### 4. Multi-Client Operation

| Score | Criteria                       |
| ----- | ------------------------------ |
| 2     | Serves 5+ clients              |
| 1     | Serves 2-4 clients             |
| 0     | Single-client or in-house team |

**Why:** Multi-client agencies need to scale processes across accounts. The AI Marketing Employee multiplies capacity per client.

### 5. Digital Maturity

| Score | Criteria                                                                         |
| ----- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2     | Uses 3+ marketing tools (analytics, CRM, ad platforms, email, social scheduling) |
| 1     | Uses 1-2 tools                                                                   |
| 0     | No visible tooling adoption                                                      |

**Why:** Digitally mature agencies understand tool value and have data infrastructure the AI Employee can plug into. Low-maturity agencies need education first.

### 6. Geography

| Score | Criteria       |
| ----- | -------------- |
| 2     | Netherlands    |
| 1     | United Kingdom |
| 0     | Other          |

**Why:** NL is primary market (Dutch language, cultural fit, legal alignment). UK is secondary (English-speaking, similar agency culture). Other markets add complexity.

## Qualification Thresholds

| Total Score | Classification | Action                                                   |
| ----------- | -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| 8-12        | Hot prospect   | Prioritize outreach, move to Touch 1 immediately         |
| 5-7         | Warm prospect  | Add to pipeline, outreach when hot prospects are covered |
| 0-4         | Skip           | Do not pursue, does not fit ICP                          |

## Anti-ICP Signals (Auto-Disqualify)

Any of the following signals disqualify an agency regardless of score:

- **Creative/branding-only agency** -- No performance or repeatable process focus. AI Employee does not fit their workflow.
- **One-person freelancer** -- No scaling need, no team to multiply, budget too constrained.
- **Already has in-house AI team** -- They build their own tools. Not a buyer, potentially a competitor.
- **Enterprise (50+ employees)** -- Different buying process (procurement, legal review, 6-month sales cycles). Not suited for founding member model.

## How to Use

1. Find agency on LinkedIn Sales Navigator
2. Check their website and LinkedIn company page
3. Score each of the 6 criteria (0-2)
4. Check for anti-ICP signals
5. If no disqualifiers and score 5+, add to `target-agencies.csv`
6. Record the ICP score in the CSV for prioritization
