## Content Strategy Notes

### Language mix

- **Dutch:** Primary for NL agency targeting. Builds trust and cultural fit with Dutch buyers who value directness ("Wat doet het? Wat kost het? Bewijs het.")
- **English:** Secondary for broader reach, UK targeting, and international authority building.
- **Ratio:** ~60% Dutch, ~40% English

### Post cadence

- 3-5 posts per week
- Comment on 5-10 prospect posts daily (substantive comments, not "Great post!")
- Reply to comments on own posts within 2 hours

### Top formats (ranked by engagement multiplier)

1. **Document carousels** (1.3-1.8x) -- step-by-step breakdowns, data visualizations
2. **Personal stories with business lessons** (1.5-2x) -- founder journey, failures, learnings
3. **Original data/research** (1.3-1.5x) -- pilot results, market analysis, benchmarks
4. **Contrarian industry takes** (1.2-1.5x) -- challenge conventional wisdom about AI, tools, hiring

### Algorithm notes (2026)

- Comments carry 15x more algorithmic weight than likes
- 3-Touch Rule before connecting: (1) comment on prospect's post, (2) reply to their comment elsewhere, (3) send connection request -- lifts response rates by 32%
- Native video outperforms YouTube links for reach
- Posts with images get 2x engagement vs text-only
