# 5-Touch LinkedIn Outreach Sequence

Complete outreach templates for the founding member campaign. All templates in Dutch (primary) and English (secondary). Use [placeholders] for personalization.

## Before You Start: The 3-Touch Rule

Never pitch cold. Before sending a connection request, complete at least one warm engagement:

1. **Comment** on their recent LinkedIn post (substantive, not "Goed punt!")
2. **Reply** to their comment on someone else's post (if opportunity arises)
3. **Connect** only after they have seen your name

This lifts connection acceptance rates by ~32% and DM response rates significantly.

## Timing Overview

| Touch | Day       | Action                 | Goal                   |
| ----- | --------- | ---------------------- | ---------------------- |
| 1     | Day 0     | Comment on their post  | Get on their radar     |
| 2     | Day 2-3   | Connection request     | Get connected          |
| 3     | Day 5-7   | Value message          | Start conversation     |
| 4     | Day 10-14 | Soft pitch             | Introduce offer        |
| 5     | Day 18-21 | Follow-up / close loop | Book call or close out |

**Volume:** Maximum 10-15 new outreach sequences per day to maintain personalization quality.

---

## Touch 1: Warm Engagement (Day 0)

**Action:** Comment on the prospect's most recent LinkedIn post.

**Rules:**

- Must reference specific content from their post
- Add genuine insight, experience, or a thoughtful question
- 2-3 sentences, no pitch, no self-promotion
- Goal: They see your name and profile

### Template (NL)

> Interessant punt over [specifiek onderwerp uit hun post]. Bij de bureaus waar ik mee werk zie ik hetzelfde patroon -- [eigen inzicht of ervaring die waarde toevoegt]. Ben benieuwd hoe jullie dat aanpakken, [voornaam].

### Template (EN)

> Interesting point about [specific topic from their post]. I've seen the same pattern with agencies I work with -- [your own insight or experience that adds value]. Curious how you're approaching this, [first name].

**Personalization checklist:**

- [ ] Referenced a specific point from their post (not generic)
- [ ] Added your own insight or perspective
- [ ] Ended with engagement (question or curiosity)
- [ ] No mention of FutureMarketingAI or your product

---

## Touch 2: Connection Request (Day 2-3)

**Action:** Send a personalized connection request (under 300 characters).

**Rules:**

- Under 300 characters
- Reference your comment or their content
- Mention what you do in one phrase, not a pitch
- No links, no attachments

### Template (NL)

> Hoi [naam], ik zag je post over [onderwerp] -- herkenbaar. Ik bouw een AI marketing medewerker specifiek voor bureaus zoals [bureau naam]. Leuk om te connecten!

### Template (EN)

> Hi [name], saw your post about [topic] -- resonated with me. Building an AI marketing employee specifically for agencies like [agency]. Would be great to connect.

**Personalization checklist:**

- [ ] Used their first name
- [ ] Referenced their specific post or topic
- [ ] Named their agency
- [ ] Under 300 characters
- [ ] No pitch, just context

---

## Touch 3: Value Message (Day 5-7)

**Action:** After connection accepted, send a value-first message. Start a conversation, do not pitch.

**Rules:**

- Share a relevant insight, article, or observation
- Ask about their experience with AI
- No mention of pricing or founding members yet
- Goal: Get them talking

### Template (NL)

> Bedankt voor de connectie, [naam]! Ik deel dit even -- [link naar relevant artikel of eigen LinkedIn post over AI voor bureaus]. Wat ik steeds vaker hoor van bureaus is dat ze wel AI tools gebruiken, maar er nog geen echte productiviteitswinst uit halen. Hoe kijken jullie bij [bureau naam] daar tegenaan?

### Template (EN)

> Thanks for connecting, [name]! Sharing something I found relevant -- [link to relevant article or your own LinkedIn post about AI for agencies]. What I keep hearing from agencies is they use AI tools but haven't unlocked real productivity gains yet. How are you thinking about that at [agency name]?

### Alternative (NL) -- If they responded to your comment

> Leuk dat je reageerde op mijn comment, [naam]! Het triggerde me om wat meer na te denken over [onderwerp]. Hoe pakt [bureau naam] dat nu aan? Bij ons zien we dat de meeste bureaus vastlopen op [relevant pijnpunt].

### Alternative (EN) -- If they responded to your comment

> Great that you replied to my comment, [name]! It got me thinking more about [topic]. How is [agency name] handling that right now? What we're seeing is most agencies hit a wall with [relevant pain point].

**Personalization checklist:**

- [ ] Thanked for connecting
- [ ] Shared something genuinely valuable (not your product)
- [ ] Asked an open question about their situation
- [ ] Referenced their agency by name
- [ ] No pitch

---

## Touch 4: Soft Pitch (Day 10-14)

**Action:** Reference the conversation so far. Introduce the founding member offer.

**Rules:**

- Only send if they responded to Touch 3 (or showed interest)
- If no response to Touch 3, skip to Touch 5
- Reference something specific from your conversation
- Mention the 10-spot scarcity and price
- Offer a 15-minute demo call, no obligation
- Attach demo video if available

### Template (NL)

> [Naam], naar aanleiding van ons gesprek over [onderwerp] -- ik denk dat dit relevant voor jullie is.

> Ik ben nu 10 founding member plekken aan het vullen voor bureaus die als eerste een AI marketing medewerker willen inzetten. Alle 6 vaardigheden inbegrepen (content, social media, ads, leads, voice, reporting) voor EUR 997/mo -- normaal EUR 1.997.

> Founding members krijgen directe lijn met mij, maandelijkse strategie-call, en de prijs ligt levenslang vast.

> Zou je een korte demo willen zien? 15 min, geen verplichtingen. [Link naar demo video of Calendly]

### Template (EN)

> [Name], following up on our conversation about [topic] -- I think this might be relevant for you.

> I'm filling 10 founding member spots for agencies that want to be first to deploy an AI marketing employee. All 6 skills included (content, social media, ads, leads, voice, reporting) at EUR 997/mo -- normally EUR 1,997.

> Founding members get a direct line with me, monthly strategy call, and locked pricing for life.

> Would you like a quick demo? 15 min, no obligations. [Link to demo video or Calendly]

**Personalization checklist:**

- [ ] Referenced previous conversation point
- [ ] Mentioned all 6 skills
- [ ] Included EUR 997 price and EUR 1,997 comparison
- [ ] Mentioned 10 spots (scarcity)
- [ ] Offered 15-min call, no obligation
- [ ] Included demo video link or booking link

---

## Touch 5: Follow-up / Close the Loop (Day 18-21)

**Action:** Final touch. Either create urgency or gracefully close out.

**Rules:**

- Adapt based on their response status
- Use honest scarcity (only state remaining spots if true)
- Respect a "no" -- do not push further
- This is the final outreach touch

### Template A: No Response (NL)

> Hoi [naam], snap dat het druk is. Wilde even laten weten dat er nog [X] van de 10 founding member plekken beschikbaar zijn. Mocht het een kwestie van timing zijn -- ik houd een plek open tot [datum, 7 dagen uit].

> Geen druk, maar wilde het je niet laten missen. Als het nu niet past, helemaal prima -- ik wens jullie bij [bureau naam] veel succes!

### Template A: No Response (EN)

> Hi [name], totally understand things get busy. Just wanted to let you know there are still [X] of 10 founding member spots available. If it's a matter of timing -- I can hold a spot until [date, 7 days out].

> No pressure at all, but didn't want you to miss it. If now isn't the right time, completely fine -- wishing you and [agency name] all the best!

### Template B: Responded But Not Committed (NL)

> [Naam], ik begreep dat je [specifieke bezwaar/vraag] noemde. Dat hoor ik vaker, en het is een terecht punt.

> [Bezwaar-specifiek antwoord -- zie objectie handling sectie hieronder]

> Zullen we even 15 minuten bellen zodat ik het kan laten zien? Dan kun je zelf beoordelen. [Calendly link]

### Template B: Responded But Not Committed (EN)

> [Name], I understood your concern about [specific objection/question]. That's a fair point and something I hear often.

> [Objection-specific response -- see objection handling section below]

> Shall we jump on a 15-min call so I can show you? You can judge for yourself. [Calendly link]

### Template C: Said No (NL)

> Helemaal begrepen, [naam]. Bedankt dat je eerlijk bent. Ik hou je op de hoogte via LinkedIn als er ontwikkelingen zijn die relevant zijn voor [bureau naam]. Succes!

### Template C: Said No (EN)

> Completely understood, [name]. Thanks for being upfront. I'll keep you in the loop via LinkedIn if there are developments relevant to [agency name]. Best of luck!

**Personalization checklist:**

- [ ] Adapted template based on response status (A, B, or C)
- [ ] Used honest scarcity numbers
- [ ] Set a specific date for deadline (not vague)
- [ ] Respected their decision if they declined
- [ ] Named their agency

## Objection Handling Guide

The 5 most common objections with responses in Dutch and English. Use these in Touch 5 (Template B) or during demo calls.

### 1. "EUR 997/mo is veel" (Price)

**NL:**

> Begrijpelijk. Ter vergelijking: een junior marketeer kost EUR 3.000-5.000 per maand, werkt 40 uur per week, heeft vakantie en wordt ziek. De AI Marketing Medewerker werkt 24/7 voor een derde van die kosten. En als founding member zit je levenslang op dit tarief -- het reguliere tarief wordt EUR 1.997/mo.

**EN:**

> Understandable. For context: a junior marketer costs EUR 3,000-5,000 per month, works 40 hours a week, takes holidays, and calls in sick. The AI Marketing Employee works 24/7 for a third of that cost. And as a founding member, you lock in this rate for life -- the regular rate is EUR 1,997/mo.

**Key angle:** Compare to hiring cost, not to tool cost. This is an employee replacement, not a SaaS subscription.

### 2. "AI kwaliteit is niet goed genoeg" (Quality)

**NL:**

> Terecht dat je dat vraagt. Laat me het je laten zien met jullie eigen klantdata. We kunnen een pilot van 2 weken doen waarbij je de output zelf beoordeelt. No strings attached. Wat je ook gaat zien: de AI is getraind op jouw merk, jouw tone-of-voice, en jouw klantdata -- het is geen generiek ChatGPT.

**EN:**

> Fair question. Let me show you with your own client data. We can do a 2-week pilot where you evaluate the output yourself. No strings attached. What you'll also see: the AI is trained on your brand, your tone of voice, and your client data -- this isn't generic ChatGPT.

**Key angle:** Offer to prove it with their data. Shift from abstract quality concern to concrete demonstration.

### 3. "Ik heb geen tijd" (Time)

**NL:**

> Dat snap ik -- en dat is precies het probleem dat we oplossen. Founding members krijgen white-glove onboarding: wij configureren alles. Jij hoeft niks te leren of in te richten. Na setup kost het je 15 minuten per maand voor een feedback call. De rest draait automatisch.

**EN:**

> I get that -- and that's exactly the problem we solve. Founding members get white-glove onboarding: we configure everything. You don't need to learn or set up anything. After setup, it takes 15 minutes per month for a feedback call. The rest runs automatically.

**Key angle:** They don't have time BECAUSE they need this. White-glove setup removes the onboarding burden.

### 4. "Wat als ik stop?" (Commitment)

**NL:**

> De minimale looptijd is 6 maanden -- genoeg om echt waarde te ervaren. Daarna is het maandelijks opzegbaar met 30 dagen opzegtermijn. Al je data blijft van jou en wordt binnen 30 dagen geexporteerd als je stopt. Geen lock-in, geen verrassingen.

**EN:**

> The minimum commitment is 6 months -- enough to truly experience the value. After that, it's month-to-month with 30 days notice. All your data stays yours and gets exported within 30 days if you leave. No lock-in, no surprises.

**Key angle:** 6 months is fair to prove value. After that, full flexibility. Data ownership is non-negotiable.

### 5. "Ik wil eerst resultaten zien" (Proof)

**NL:**

> Helemaal fair. We werken nu met onze eerste klant (e-commerce merk) en de resultaten zijn veelbelovend. Maar eerlijk: daarom zoeken we founding members. Jullie krijgen de laagste prijs ooit in ruil voor het feit dat jullie mee helpen het product te vormen. Na 30 dagen kijken we samen of het waarde oplevert -- zo niet, dan praten we.

**EN:**

> Completely fair. We're working with our first client (e-commerce brand) now and results are promising. But honestly: that's exactly why we're looking for founding members. You get the lowest price ever in exchange for helping shape the product. After 30 days, we evaluate together whether it's delivering value -- if not, we talk.

**Key angle:** Acknowledge early stage honestly. Founding member status IS the proof opportunity. Frame it as mutual investment.

## General Outreach Principles

1. **Personalize every message.** Reference their agency name, their recent posts, their clients, their team size. Generic messages get ignored.
2. **Never pitch in the first two touches.** Build rapport first. People buy from people they know.
3. **Use urgency honestly.** Only state remaining spots if the number is accurate. Dutch buyers detect fake scarcity instantly.
4. **Match their language.** If their LinkedIn is in Dutch, write in Dutch. If English, write in English.
5. **Track everything.** Update `target-agencies.csv` after every touch. Record objections and notes.
6. **DMs are for booking calls, not closing.** Complex B2B sales at EUR 997/mo require a conversation. Push to a 15-minute video call.
7. **Respect a "no."** One graceful close-out message (Template C), then move on. Reputation matters in a small market like NL.
8. **Volume over perfection.** Send 10-15 quality outreach sequences per day. Consistency beats optimization.
