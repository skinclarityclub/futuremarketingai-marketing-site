# Competitive Pricing Analysis — FMai vs Market

## TL;DR Verdict

**EUR 1,000-2,000/mo is significantly underpriced for what FMai delivers.** The research shows:

- Replacing FMai's capabilities with individual SaaS tools costs EUR 1,500-3,500/mo in software alone (no human labor)
- A single AI SDR employee (just outbound sales, one skill) costs $5,000/mo (11x Alice)
- A human social media manager in the Netherlands costs EUR 4,500-6,200/mo salary
- Full-service marketing agency retainers in Europe run EUR 5,000-20,000/mo
- Value-based pricing for AI agents is moving toward outcome-based models at 3-10x current SaaS pricing

**Recommended price range: EUR 2,500-5,000/mo** for full-stack AI Marketing Employee, with EUR 7,500-15,000/mo for enterprise/agency tiers.

- **FMai current pricing**: EUR 297-1,997/mo (72-95% cheaper)

## 5. Value-Based Pricing for AI Agents — Industry Direction

### The Shift Away from Per-Seat Pricing

The industry is moving rapidly:
- **Usage-based pricing** reached 61% adoption by 2022
- **Outcome-based models** expected to reach ~30% adoption by 2025
- **Gartner prediction**: By 2030, 40% of enterprise SaaS spend shifts to usage-, agent-, or outcome-based pricing

### Three Emerging Models for AI Agents

1. **Higher per-seat price** — If AI makes one seat as productive as 3 humans, charge 3x (e.g., $500-1,500/seat instead of $150/seat)
2. **Usage-based** — Per API call, per content piece, per minute of voice
3. **Outcome-based** — Per lead generated, per meeting booked, per conversion

### What This Means for FMai

FMai's AI Marketing Employee replaces 2-4 human roles. The value comparison:
- **2 junior marketing employees (NL)**: EUR 5,000-7,000/mo in salary alone
- **1 social media manager + 1 content creator**: EUR 8,000-11,000/mo
- **Agency retainer for equivalent scope**: EUR 5,000-20,000/mo

**Value-based pricing anchor: EUR 3,000-5,000/mo** (50-70% discount vs human equivalent)

## 6. Pricing Recommendations

### Current Pricing Assessment

| Current Tier | Current Price | Market Value | Underpriced By |
|-------------|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| Starter (EUR 297/mo) | Basic automation | EUR 500-1,000/mo | 40-70% |
| Growth (EUR 997/mo) | Multi-skill AI employee | EUR 2,500-4,000/mo | 60-75% |
| Scale (EUR 1,997/mo) | Full-stack AI employee | EUR 5,000-10,000/mo | 60-80% |

### Recommended Pricing Tiers

Based on competitive analysis and value-based pricing:

| Tier | Recommended Price | Justification |
|------|------------------|---------------|
| **Starter** | **EUR 997/mo** | Single skill (Content OR Social), comparable to Jasper + Hootsuite + human time |
| **Professional** | **EUR 2,497/mo** | 3-4 skills, comparable to one junior marketing employee |
| **Business** | **EUR 4,997/mo** | All skills, comparable to 11x Alice ($5K/mo) but with 6x the capabilities |
| **Enterprise** | **EUR 7,500-15,000/mo** | Custom, multi-brand, dedicated support, comparable to agency retainer |

### Price Anchoring Strategy

When selling, anchor against:
1. **Human replacement cost**: "A junior marketer in NL costs EUR 4,500/mo + benefits. Clyde costs EUR 2,497/mo, works 24/7, and does the work of 3 juniors."
2. **Tool stack cost**: "The software alone to replicate this costs EUR 2,000-3,400/mo. And you still need someone to run it all."
3. **AI employee comparables**: "11x charges $5,000/mo for just an AI SDR. Clyde does content, social, ads, voice, leads, AND reporting for the same price."
4. **Agency retainer comparison**: "A full-service agency charges EUR 5,000-20,000/mo. Clyde delivers comparable output for a fraction, with 24/7 availability."
