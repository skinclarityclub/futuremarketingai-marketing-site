### 🔬 Best Practices 2025 Research

**Key Principles:**
1. **Context-Aware Translations**: Don't translate word-for-word. Adapt to cultural context.
2. **Tone Consistency**: Match brand voice in each language
3. **RTL Ready**: Prepare structure for future RTL languages
4. **Pluralization**: Use i18next pluralization features
5. **Gender Neutrality**: Where applicable, use inclusive language
6. **SEO Optimization**: Proper meta tags per language
7. **Dynamic Content**: Handle dates, numbers, currency correctly
8. **Fallback Chain**: en → nl → es hierarchy

**Translation Quality Guidelines:**
- ✅ Natural flow in target language
- ✅ Industry-appropriate terminology
- ✅ Consistent UI/UX terminology across app
- ✅ No machine translation feel
- ✅ Test with native speakers
