## 4. SEO & Distribution Strategy

### **On-Site SEO:**
- Blog at `/blog` or `/insights`
- Proper meta tags, Open Graph
- Internal linking strategy
- Semantic HTML (article, h1-h6)
- Image optimization (WebP, alt text)
- Schema.org markup (Article, Person)

### **Distribution Channels:**
1. **LinkedIn** (primary for B2B):
   - Post article link + thread
   - Personal profile (Daley)
   - Company page
   - Relevant groups

2. **Twitter/X**:
   - Thread version of article
   - Tag relevant accounts
   - Use hashtags: #AIMarketing #MarketingAutomation

3. **Hacker News** (for technical posts):
   - Submit Show HN
   - Engage in comments

4. **Reddit**:
   - r/marketing
   - r/Entrepreneur
   - r/SaaS (be authentic, not spammy)

5. **Newsletter**:
   - Weekly digest
   - Grow email list
   - Segment by interest

6. **Guest Posts**:
   - Pitch to industry blogs
   - CMSWire, MarTech, VentureBeat
