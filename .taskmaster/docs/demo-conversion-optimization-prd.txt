# Future Marketing AI Demo - Conversion Optimization PRD

## Executive Summary

Transform the Future Marketing AI demo into a highly converting, interactive experience that showcases custom-made solutions, ongoing AI advisory services, and measurable ROI. The demo must guide prospects from awareness to booking a strategic consultation through personalized interactions and clear value propositions.

## Business Objectives

1. **Increase Demo-to-Lead Conversion** from visitors to consultation bookings
2. **Communicate Custom Solutions** - Make "maatwerk" (custom-made) messaging central
3. **Highlight AI Advisory Service** - Position ongoing partnership as key differentiator
4. **Demonstrate Clear ROI** - Use interactive calculator with visitor's own data
5. **Reduce Friction** - Make next steps obvious and easy at every stage

## Target Audience

- **Primary**: Business Owners (SME, 10-100 employees) in e-commerce, services, B2B
- **Secondary**: Marketing Managers seeking automation and efficiency gains
- **Pain Points**: Time-consuming content creation, multi-platform management, measuring ROI, staying current with trends

## Technical Constraints & Considerations

- **Performance Budget**: Total bundle size < 500KB (gzipped)
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
- **Browser Support**: Chrome/Edge/Safari/Firefox (last 2 versions)
- **Mobile**: iOS Safari, Android Chrome
- **Load Time**: FCP < 1.5s, LCP < 2.5s
- **Animations**: Respect prefers-reduced-motion

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Calculator too complex | High | A/B test simplified version, progressive disclosure |
| Exit intent annoying | Medium | Limit to 1x per session, easy dismiss |
| Calendly integration fails | High | Fallback contact form, test thoroughly |
| Mobile performance poor | High | Aggressive lazy loading, reduce 3D complexity on mobile |
| Users don't understand "maatwerk" | Medium | Add tooltip, use "custom-made" in English too |
